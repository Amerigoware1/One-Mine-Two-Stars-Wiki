Overview

Ananya Patel moved from Utopis to New Eden when she was nine. Direct and energetic, she treats Celectra's extraordinary life like regular teenage drama. Known for her sharp humor and disciplined scientific mind, she volunteers to ease Celectra's burden and becomes a trained Listener Corps translator while pursuing her education in biology.

Career & Education

Biologist (in training) - At 22 Earth years, Ananya is a recent graduate in xenobiology, applying her scientific training to the study of Galinstanian life and communication. Her disciplined approach to observation and analysis makes her particularly effective in cross-species research.

Listener Corps Translator - As one of the first volunteers for the Listener Corps, Ananya works alongside Chen Wei and others to develop sustainable communication methods, helping to build the foundation that will eventually replace Celectra's biological bridge.
