Background

Jenni Carisco was an exogeologist stationed on Utopis-Lab 3, orbiting the moon Oscar in the Ares system. She discovered she was pregnant during a survey mission, approximately six weeks along, and chose to keep the news secret until she could tell her husband, Amerigo Carisco, in person upon her return. However, a solar mass ejection from the star Niamh caused a propulsion anomaly on the station, exposing the crew‚Äîand Jenni‚Äîto high levels of radiation. Personality and Traits

Jenni is depicted as a resilient and determined scientist, facing the uncertainties of her pregnancy and radiation exposure with analytical pragmatism. She prioritizes protecting her unborn child(ren), displaying a protective maternal instinct even in the face of dire medical prognoses. Her scientific background shines through in her methodical approach to crises, calculating risks and outcomes like geological surveys.

Key traits include:

  • Resilience: Jenni endures the physical and emotional toll of radiation exposure without losing hope.
  • Protectiveness: She instinctively shields her abdomen during the flare event and commits to monitoring the pregnancy despite poor odds.
  • Analytical: Approaches her situation with data-driven logic, questioning medical certainties and seeking any possible positive outcomes.
  • Optimism: Holds onto hope for her child's survival, whispering encouragement to her unborn twins.

To further develop her character, Jenni could be shown in flashbacks as a pioneering explorer, driven by curiosity about extraterrestrial geology, which mirrors the story's themes of discovery and adaptation.

Relationships

Amerigo Carisco (Husband)

Jenni shares a deep, loving bond with Amerigo, dreaming of their future family together. She plans to surprise him with the pregnancy news in person, highlighting their intimate connection.

Celectra Carisco (Daughter)

As Celectra's birth mother, Jenni's protective actions during the flare directly influence her daughter's unique physiology. Though she does not survive long after the birth, her legacy lives on through Celectra's existence.

Dr. Yuki Chen (Colleague)

Jenni interacts professionally with Dr. Chen, who provides medical care and honest assessments during her pregnancy, offering compassion amid uncertainty.
