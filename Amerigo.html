Overview

Amerigo Carisco is a materials scientist, engineer, and one of the original colonists of the New Eden settlement in the Chaos system. He is the father of Celectra Carisco and the widower of exogeologist Jenni Carisco. He played a central role in the discovery of the Galinstanians and in protecting his daughter against the unethical experimentation of Dr. Douglas Kret.
