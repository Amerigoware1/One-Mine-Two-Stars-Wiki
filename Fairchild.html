Overview

Terry Fairchild is a senior nurse with over two decades of medical experience who served as Celectra Carisco's primary caregiver during Dr. Douglas Kret's unethical experimentation period in 2232. Of multiracial heritage from Durban, South Africa, Fairchild presents with intentionally neutral features that reflect their non-binary identity. Despite being employed by Dr. Kret, they maintained strong ethical boundaries and provided crucial compassionate care to the traumatized seven-year-old Celectra, ultimately playing a key role in exposing Kret's misconduct.

Appearance & Demeanor

As observed by Celectra during their first meeting: "Their features were pleasantly neutral, neither conventionally masculine nor feminine, and their short hair offered no additional clues." Fairchild's professional appearance reflects their intentional rejection of gender binaries, with Dr. Kret's correct use of they/them pronouns noted by Celectra as "at least basic respect."

Role During Kret's Experiments

Fairchild served as Dr. Kret's assistant during his unethical research on Celectra's mononous disoma condition, but frequently found themselves torn between professional duty and moral conviction.

  • Compassionate Caregiver: Regularly brought Celectra breakfast in bed, played Neutralize with her (losing three consecutive matches), and provided emotional support
  • Early Recognition: Quickly understood Celectra was one person across two bodies after observing her terror at separation
  • Moral Conflict: Displayed visible discomfort during procedures, hesitating when ordered to restrain Celectra
  • Critical Intervention: During the catastrophic separation experiment, Fairchild's composure cracked as they screamed "She's DEAD!" and demanded the chamber be opened
  • Life-Saving Action: Performed CPR on Celeste's body while Dr. Kret checked Electra, demonstrating their medical skill under extreme pressure

Ethical Stance & Professional Impact

Fairchild's experience with Kret fundamentally shaped their approach to medical ethics in New Eden:

  • Patient Advocacy: Became a vocal advocate for patient autonomy and consent, especially for vulnerable populations
  • Trauma-Informed Care: Developed protocols for medical care of patients with unique physiological conditions
  • Whistleblower Role: Their testimony, combined with Utara's records, helped convict Dr. Kret
  • Mentorship: Now trains younger medical staff on ethical decision-making and boundary maintenance
  • Colony Trust: Earned respect from the Carisco family and broader community for their integrity under pressure

As they told Amerigo Carisco: "I don't believe he intended to harm her, but he showed complete disregard for her safety." This distinction between malice and reckless arrogance informed colony medical ethics reforms.
