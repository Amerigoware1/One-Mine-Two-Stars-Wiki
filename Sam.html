Sam

Sam on a neutral presentation day
Full Name Sam (born Samantha) Pena
Age 19
Gender Genderfluid
Pronouns They/them (accepts she/her or he/him based on presentation)
Origin Utopis Space Station

Occupation Station Maintenance Technician / Aspiring Cultural Liaison

Overview

Sam is a young genderfluid resident who relocated from Utopis Space Station to New Eden in search of independence and self-discovery. Born with congenital atrichia, they have embraced their unique appearance as part of their fluid identity, using fashion and headwear to express their mood and presentation. Known for their warmth, curiosity, and emotional intelligence, Sam forms deep connections easily and serves as a bridge between diverse cultural backgrounds.

Physical Appearance

Sam has congenital atrichia universalis, meaning they have no body hair, eyebrows, or eyelashes from birth. Their smooth, reflective scalp is often accentuated with oils, shimmer, or elaborate headwear. They possess a slender, wiry build adapted to low-gravity station life, granting them notable agility. Sam's eyes are strikingly expressive, conveying emotions through subtle micro-expressions that others quickly learn to read.

Their presentation shifts fluidly: feminine days feature flowing skirts, pastel wigs, and layered jewelry; masculine days emphasize sharp lines, structured clothing, and a bare, polished scalp; neutral days favor oversized utility wear or jumpsuits with minimal adornment. A signature collection of scarves, wraps, circlets, and metallic bands serves as a visual indicator of their current mood.

Diagnosis:

Congenital Atrichia Universalis

Etiology:

Genetic condition (non-syndromic)

Primary Risk:

Increased sun sensitivity; requires protective measures on planetary surfaces

Secondary Risks:

None significant; condition is cosmetic and non-progressive

Background

  • Born and raised on Utopis Space Station in a tight-knit, diverse community.
  • Grew up surrounded by maintenance crews, learning basic engineering skills from an early age.
  • Experienced protective but loving family dynamics; felt constantly "known" in the small station environment.
  • Relocated to New Eden seeking anonymity, professional opportunities, and freedom to explore identity without preconceptions.
  • Motivated by a desire to escape being defined as "the atrichia kid" or limited by station expectations.

Personality

  • Deeply curious about identity, consciousness, and cultural differences between humans and Galinstanians.
  • Warm and non-judgmental; people feel safe opening up to them.
  • Restless searcher for meaning, genuine connection, and a true sense of home.
  • Emotionally resilient yet vulnerable to fears of misunderstanding, fetishization, or pity.
  • Highly empathetic with strong emotional intelligence; excels at reading masked feelings and bridging misunderstandings.

Skills and Talents

  • Practical station engineering and maintenance.
  • Improvisational fashion and styling expertise.
  • Cultural fluency from Utopis' diverse population.
  • Exceptional communication and conflict mediation.
  • Agility and quick reflexes adapted to low-gravity environments.

Personal Life

Sam is currently building a new life in New Eden, forming meaningful relationships outside the confines of their childhood community. They have developed a close, tender connection with Maya, marked by mutual healing and growth. Sam's fluidity encourages Maya to explore her own identity, while Maya's steadiness and acceptance provide the grounding Sam craves.

Sam identifies as attracted exclusively to women and navigates relationships with openness and vulnerability, seeking partners who allow them to define themselves without pressure.
