Overview

Dr. Alexander "Alex" Cruiz is the Chief of Medicine for New Eden Colony and Amerigo Carisco's closest friend. A calm, compassionate physician with a keen scientific mind, he played a crucial role in Celectra Carisco's medical care and later succeeded Dr. Douglas Kret as Chief of Medicine after Kret's ethical violations. Known affectionately as "Uncle Doc" to Celectra and "Uncle Alex" to the wider family, he represents the ethical, patient-centered approach to medicine that Kret abandoned.

Alex and his wife Dee relocated from Utopis Station to New Eden after his promotion, moving into the house opposite the Cariscos in Zo√´'s former residence, cementing their role as extended family.

Role in Celectra's Care (Book 1 - 2232)

Initial Contact

  • Became Celectra's primary physician during her childhood on Utopis Station
  • Celectra affectionately called him "Uncle Doc," reflecting his dual role as physician and family friend
  • Provided compassionate care during her traumatic experiences with Dr. Kret's experiments
  • Witnessed Kret's unethical procedures but lacked the authority to intervene directly at the time

The Second Incident

  • When Amerigo was called away to Utopis-Lab 2, Celectra was left in Alex and Dee's care
  • Despite their supervision, Kret used political manipulation to gain access to Celectra
  • Alex and Dee carried guilt for years over their perceived failure to protect Celectra during this incident
  • Celectra later absolved them of this guilt, recognizing the systemic power dynamics at play

Promotion to Chief of Medicine

Following Dr. Kret's first tribunal in 2232 for his unethical experiments on Celectra:

  • Kret was stripped of his position as Chief of Medicine
  • Alex was unanimously appointed as his successor by the colony council
  • The promotion mandated his relocation from Utopis Station to New Eden on Ares
  • Dee Cruiz relocated with him, leaving her botanical work on Utopis to establish new projects in New Eden
  • They moved into Zo√´ Carisco's former house, directly opposite the Carisco residence across the courtyard

Council Membership

Medical Philosophy

Alex's approach to medicine stands in direct contrast to his predecessor:

  • Patient-centered care: Always prioritizes the well-being and autonomy of the patient
  • Ethical rigor: Maintains strict adherence to medical ethics, even when research opportunities are tempting
  • Holistic perspective: Considers psychological and social factors alongside physical health
  • Preventive focus: Emphasizes prevention and community health over dramatic interventions
  • Collaborative practice: Works closely with other specialists, including Zo√´'s psychological expertise

Role in Key Medical Events

Celectra's Unique Physiology

  • Oversaw Celectra's medical care throughout her childhood and adolescence
  • Documented her mononous disoma condition with rigorous ethical standards
  • Monitored her Celectricity development and its physiological correlates
  • Provided care during her recovery from Kret's traumatic experiments

Galinstanian Medicine

  • Pioneered the first cross-species medical protocols
  • Developed techniques for assessing Galinstanian health and well-being
  • Served as medical expert during the northern cluster rescue operation
  • Testified about Galinstanian trauma at Kret's second tribunal

Celectra's Pregnancy (Book 3)

  • Primary physician for Celectra's pregnancy with twins
  • Managed the unprecedented medical situation of a dual-body pregnancy
  • Discovered and monitored the twin pregnancy (Mara Zo and Jenni Jo)
  • Oversaw the delivery and postpartum care for both Celectra's bodies
  • Documented the unique physiological aspects for future medical reference

Relationship with Dee Cruiz

Alex and Dee's marriage represents one of the colony's longest-standing relationships:

  • Married during their university years on Earth
  • Both committed to the colonization vision and applied together
  • Support each other's professional work while maintaining a strong partnership
  • Often work collaboratively on projects combining medicine and botany
  • Known as "Uncle Alex and Aunt Dee" to the Carisco children and many in the colony

Their childlessness by choice has allowed them to focus on their careers and their role as "colony aunt and uncle" to many, particularly the Carisco family.

Personality and Traits

  • Calm under pressure: Maintains composure in medical emergencies
  • Compassionate: Genuinely cares for his patients' well-being
  • Ethical: Unwavering commitment to medical ethics
  • Loyal Deeply committed to his friends and family
  • Professionally thorough: Meticulous in documentation and procedure
  • Adaptable: Has adjusted to frontier medicine and alien physiology
  • Humble Despite his position, avoids self-aggrandizement

Legacy and Impact

Dr. Alex Cruiz's legacy includes:

  • Restoring ethical standards to colonial medicine after Kret's violations
  • Establishing protocols for cross-species medical care
  • Documenting unique medical cases (Celectra's condition, dual-body pregnancy) for future reference
  • Training the next generation of colonial physicians
  • Providing a model of compassionate, patient-centered care
  • Maintaining one of the colony's most enduring friendships (with Amerigo)
  • Being honored through his namesake, Xan Carisco

His steady, ethical presence provides medical and moral stability to New Eden Colony, representing the best of what colonial medicine can be when guided by compassion rather than obsession.
