Overview

New Eden is the first permanent settlement established on the surface of the planet orbiting the Niamh-Ciara system. Designed to house the initial wave of colonists after stasis, it remains the administrative and symbolic heart of the colony. Though small in population, its infrastructure supports interplanetary communication, governance, and early-stage terraforming efforts.

Layout and Structures

The village is a blend of old and new: modular colony units from the original Utopis ship are nestled between newer dwellings of stone, steel, and glass. These structures cluster around a central council hall, forming a tight-knit community. New Eden Observatory

Perched on a ridge overlooking the settlement, the New Eden Observatory is a sleek dome reserved for guided observations and astronomical research. Its glass panels gleam under the binary sunset, reflecting the golden light of Niamh and the deepening pink of Ciara. The path to the dome winds through red-dust trails and scattered rocks, offering a panoramic view of the village below.

Access is restricted to authorized personnel and scheduled tours. Inside, a circular observation platform sits beneath a transparent roof, offering an unobstructed view of the sky.
