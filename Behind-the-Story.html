What This Story Is (And Isn't)

  • This is NOT a story about conjoined twins or meant to represent their experience.
  • This IS a work of science fiction that uses that biological framework to explore questions of identity, autonomy, and connection from a completely different angle.
  • This IS a story born from curiosity and respect for the diversity of human experience.

My deepest respect and gratitude go to real twins like the Hensels, whose lives and voices illuminated the path to asking my own, fictional questions.
